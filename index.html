Why Lostgrad?

Having come out of university, we needed something to help us jump into the working world - that could be embarking on a start-up, deciding on a career, or just getting a job. This can be an isolating position, so we also needed to connect with others in the same boat - bounce ideas off one another.

We are building something that we would have found beneficial to ourselves both as students going through education, and as graduates or drop-outs needing some help!

We're raising funds for a new online community!

Lostgrad wants to help students progress in all aspects of their lives by providing opportunities to enhance their skills and experiences.

Members will have free access to online courses, jobs, work experience and travel opportunities. We also provide material to inspire and provoke discussion on important topics, whilst providing advice aswell.

Where are we going?

We want to build a system to help you connect to, and help one another, whilst also keeping track of your achievements - both online and in real life!
